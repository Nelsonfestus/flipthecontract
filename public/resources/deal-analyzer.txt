=== TIPS FOR ACCURATE ANALYSIS ===

1. Always verify comps — don't rely on Zillow estimates alone
2. When in doubt, overestimate repairs by 10-15%
3. Your assignment fee should be at least $5,000 for SFR
4. Build relationships with contractors for free estimates
5. Use the 70% rule as your baseline — adjust based on market
6. Hot markets may allow 75% rule; cold markets use 65%
7. Run numbers on EVERY deal before making an offer
8. Trust the numbers, not your emotions
