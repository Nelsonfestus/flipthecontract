===============================================
  FLIP THE CONTRACT — SAMPLE CONTRACT PACKET
  PSA, Assignment & JV Agreement Samples
===============================================

This packet contains sample templates for the three most common
wholesale real estate contracts. These are for EDUCATIONAL PURPOSES
ONLY. Always consult a real estate attorney in your state before
using any contract in a live transaction.


===============================================
  1. PURCHASE AND SALE AGREEMENT (PSA)
  Standard Wholesale Contract Template
===============================================

REAL ESTATE PURCHASE AND SALE AGREEMENT

Date: _______________

SELLER:
Name: _______________________________________________
Address: ____________________________________________
Phone: ______________________________________________
Email: ______________________________________________

BUYER:
Name: _______________________________________________ and/or assigns
Address: ____________________________________________
Phone: ______________________________________________
Email: ______________________________________________

PROPERTY:
Address: ____________________________________________
City: _________________ State: _______ Zip: ________
County: _____________________________________________
Legal Description: __________________________________
Parcel/Tax ID: ______________________________________

1. PURCHASE PRICE AND TERMS
   Purchase Price: $_______________
   Earnest Money Deposit: $_______________
   Earnest Money to be held by: _______________________
   Balance due at closing: $_______________

2. CLOSING
   Closing shall occur on or before _____________ (date)
   at a mutually agreed upon title company or attorney's office.
   Closing costs shall be split as follows:
   - Seller pays: deed preparation, prorated taxes, any existing liens
   - Buyer pays: title search, title insurance, recording fees

3. INSPECTION PERIOD
   Buyer shall have _____ business days from the date of this
   agreement to conduct inspections of the property. If Buyer is
   not satisfied with the results of inspections, Buyer may
   terminate this agreement and receive a full refund of earnest
   money deposit.

4. TITLE
   Seller shall convey marketable title by warranty deed (or
   special warranty deed / quitclaim deed as applicable), free and
   clear of all liens, encumbrances, and defects, except:
   - Current year property taxes (prorated at closing)
   - Existing easements and restrictions of record

5. CONDITION OF PROPERTY
   Property is sold "AS IS, WHERE IS" with no warranties or
   representations by Seller regarding the condition of the
   property, except as specifically stated herein.

6. ASSIGNMENT
   Buyer shall have the right to assign this agreement to a
   third party without the consent of Seller. Upon assignment,
   Buyer shall be released from all obligations hereunder.

7. DEFAULT
   If Buyer defaults, Seller's sole remedy shall be retention
   of the earnest money deposit as liquidated damages.
   If Seller defaults, Buyer may seek specific performance or
   return of earnest money deposit.

8. CONTINGENCIES
   This agreement is contingent upon:
   [ ] Clear title
   [ ] Satisfactory inspection
   [ ] Buyer's ability to assign or secure funding
   [ ] Other: ________________________________________

9. ADDITIONAL TERMS
   ___________________________________________________
   ___________________________________________________
   ___________________________________________________

SELLER SIGNATURE: _________________________ Date: _________
SELLER SIGNATURE: _________________________ Date: _________

BUYER SIGNATURE: __________________________ Date: _________
BUYER SIGNATURE: __________________________ Date: _________


===============================================
  2. ASSIGNMENT OF CONTRACT
  Transfer Your Contract to an End Buyer
===============================================

ASSIGNMENT OF REAL ESTATE PURCHASE AND SALE AGREEMENT

Date: _______________

ASSIGNOR (Original Buyer):
Name: _______________________________________________
Address: ____________________________________________

ASSIGNEE (New Buyer):
Name: _______________________________________________
Address: ____________________________________________

REFERENCE CONTRACT:
Original Purchase and Sale Agreement dated: _______________
Property Address: ____________________________________________
Original Seller: ____________________________________________
Original Purchase Price: $_______________

1. ASSIGNMENT
   For good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency
   of which is hereby acknowledged, Assignor hereby assigns,
   transfers, and sets over to Assignee all of Assignor's right,
   title, and interest in and to the above-referenced Purchase and
   Sale Agreement (the "Agreement").

2. ASSIGNMENT FEE
   Assignee agrees to pay Assignor an assignment fee of
   $________________, payable as follows:
   - Non-refundable deposit of $__________ due upon execution
     of this Assignment
   - Balance of $__________ due at closing through the title
     company or closing attorney

3. ASSUMPTION OF OBLIGATIONS
   Assignee hereby assumes all obligations and duties of Assignor
   under the Agreement, including but not limited to the obligation
   to close on the property per the terms of the Agreement.

4. CLOSING
   Closing shall occur per the terms of the original Agreement.
   Assignee shall be responsible for all buyer closing costs.

5. ASSIGNOR'S REPRESENTATIONS
   Assignor represents and warrants that:
   a) The Agreement is in full force and effect
   b) Assignor has the right to assign the Agreement
   c) Assignor has not previously assigned the Agreement
   d) There are no defaults under the Agreement

6. INDEMNIFICATION
   Assignee shall indemnify and hold Assignor harmless from any
   claims, damages, or expenses arising from Assignee's failure
   to perform under the Agreement after this Assignment.

7. BINDING EFFECT
   This Assignment shall be binding upon and inure to the benefit
   of the parties hereto and their respective heirs, successors,
   and assigns.

ASSIGNOR SIGNATURE: _________________________ Date: _________

ASSIGNEE SIGNATURE: _________________________ Date: _________


===============================================
  3. JOINT VENTURE (JV) AGREEMENT
  Partner on Deals with Other Wholesalers
===============================================

JOINT VENTURE AGREEMENT FOR REAL ESTATE WHOLESALE TRANSACTION

Date: _______________

PARTY A (Deal Finder):
Name: _______________________________________________
Address: ____________________________________________
Phone: ______________________________________________
Email: ______________________________________________

PARTY B (Disposition Partner):
Name: _______________________________________________
Address: ____________________________________________
Phone: ______________________________________________
Email: ______________________________________________

PROPERTY:
Address: ____________________________________________
City: _________________ State: _______ Zip: ________

1. PURPOSE
   The parties enter into this Joint Venture Agreement for the
   sole purpose of wholesaling the above-referenced property.
   This agreement covers only this specific transaction and does
   not create an ongoing partnership or business entity.

2. ROLES AND RESPONSIBILITIES

   PARTY A shall be responsible for:
   [ ] Securing the property under contract with the seller
   [ ] Providing all property details, photos, and comps
   [ ] Managing the seller relationship through closing
   [ ] Other: ________________________________________

   PARTY B shall be responsible for:
   [ ] Marketing the deal to their buyer list
   [ ] Finding a qualified end buyer
   [ ] Coordinating with the title company for closing
   [ ] Other: ________________________________________

3. PROFIT SPLIT
   Total assignment fee / wholesale profit: $_______________

   Party A shall receive: _____% ($________________)
   Party B shall receive: _____% ($________________)

   Profits shall be disbursed through the title company at
   closing. Each party's share shall be listed on the
   settlement statement.

4. EARNEST MONEY
   Earnest money deposit of $__________ shall be provided by:
   [ ] Party A   [ ] Party B   [ ] Split equally
   In the event the deal does not close, earnest money shall be
   returned to the party who deposited it.

5. CONFIDENTIALITY
   Both parties agree to keep the terms of this agreement and
   all deal details confidential. Neither party shall disclose
   the other party's buyer list, seller contacts, or profit
   amounts to third parties.

6. NON-CIRCUMVENT
   Both parties agree not to circumvent this agreement by
   contacting the other party's seller, buyer, or contacts
   directly for the purpose of excluding the other party from
   the transaction or future transactions.

7. TERM AND TERMINATION
   This agreement shall remain in effect until the property
   closes or the purchase agreement expires/is terminated,
   whichever occurs first. Either party may terminate this
   agreement with written notice if the other party fails to
   perform their responsibilities within a reasonable time.

8. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
   Any disputes shall be resolved through mediation. If
   mediation fails, disputes shall be resolved through binding
   arbitration in the county where the property is located.

9. GOVERNING LAW
   This agreement shall be governed by the laws of the state
   of _________________________.

PARTY A SIGNATURE: _________________________ Date: _________

PARTY B SIGNATURE: _________________________ Date: _________
